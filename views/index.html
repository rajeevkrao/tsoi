Our mission

Bringing a paradigm shift in
your idea of India

Slices of inspiration and hope are required more than ever now and Thousand Shades of India (TSOI) strives hard to bring you that and more, amidst all the chaos of negativity and hopelessness.

We are a team of two experienced journalists who have quit our jobs to dedicate our lives to the cause of sharing stories that make a real difference - stories that are not covered by the mainstream media.
